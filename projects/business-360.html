Business Insights 360

Business Insights 360 is a comprehensive analytics solution designed to deliver key performance insights across multiple business domains, including Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Supply Chain.

The project leverages Power BI to build dynamic dashboards and uses DAX Studio to optimize DAX queries, improving report performance and user experience.

Objective

To develop an intuitive dashboard that consolidates key business metrics for executives and decision-makers, providing actionable insights into net sales, gross margins, customer performance, product analysis, and operational efficiency across regions and categories.

Highlights

  • Consolidated dashboards for Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive views.
  • Optimized DAX performance using DAX Studio, reducing report load time.
  • Year-over-year benchmarking for Net Sales and Gross Margin.
  • Regional and sub-region performance analysis.
  • Top and bottom customer and product analysis by net sales.
  • Performance matrix for Gross Margin % and Net Profit.
  • Forecast accuracy and net error tracking for supply chain risk management.
  • Dynamic filters for regions, customers, products, and time periods.

Conclusion

Business Insights 360 delivers a robust, data-driven analytics platform that enables leadership to monitor enterprise performance from a single source of truth.

By combining Power BI, optimized DAX, and executive-ready visualizations, this project demonstrates strong capabilities in handling complex datasets and delivering impactful business insights.
