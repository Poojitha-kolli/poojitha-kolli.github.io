Supply Chain & Finance Analytics

This project uses MySQL to perform in-depth finance and supply chain analytics for AtliQ Hardware. It analyzes key business areas such as sales performance, market trends, customer contribution, and demand forecasting, delivering actionable insights to support financial planning and supply chain decision-making.

Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze sales performance, financial metrics, and supply chain efficiency for Atliq Hardware across multiple sales channels and markets. The analysis aimed to provide actionable insights into revenue trends, profitability, customer contribution, demand forecasting accuracy, and operational performance to support data-driven business decisions.

Highlights

  • Analyzed gross sales, net sales, and discount impact to understand overall financial performance.
  • Identified top customers, markets, and products based on revenue and profitability.
  • Evaluated pre-invoice and post-invoice discounts to assess their effect on net sales.
  • Built supply chain analytics comparing actual sales vs forecasted demand to measure forecast accuracy.
  • Highlighted underperforming markets and customers based on forecast errors and sales contribution.
  • Performance matrix for Gross Margin % and Net Profit.
  • Forecast accuracy and net error tracking for supply chain risk management.
  • Used advanced SQL concepts such as CTEs, views, window functions, stored procedures, and performance optimization techniques.

Conclusion

The AtliQ Hardware: Finance & Supply Chain Analytics project focuses on analyzing sales, discounts, profitability, and market performance, along with evaluating demand versus forecast accuracy using MySQL. The project combines financial metrics and supply chain indicators to identify revenue drivers, assess discount impact, and highlight forecasting gaps. These insights help improve financial planning, operational efficiency, and data-driven decision-making
